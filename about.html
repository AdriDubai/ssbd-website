We Are Same Same But Different

Same Same But Different started with a simple idea: events in Dubai deserve better sound and lighting — delivered by people who genuinely care about the craft, not just the invoice.

We're three partners who met through the events industry in the UAE. Two of us are lighting designers, one is a sound engineer. Together, we bring over a decade of combined experience in live events — from underground music nights to high-profile corporate launches.

We launched SSBD in 2023 because we saw a gap in the market. Most AV companies in Dubai are large operations that treat sound and lighting as line items on a spreadsheet. We wanted to offer something different — a boutique service where every event gets the attention it deserves, where the people you talk to are the same people who show up on event day.

Our name says it all: every event may seem similar on the surface, but the details make each one unique. We obsess over those details — the way a spotlight catches a speaker at just the right moment, the clarity of a vocalist in a reverberant ballroom, the seamless transition from dinner ambience to dance floor energy.

Our Approach

Our Philosophy

We believe that sound and lighting are not afterthoughts — they're core elements of any event experience. When done right, they're invisible. Nobody points at a speaker and says "great sound." But everyone feels it. They feel the energy of a room, the clarity of a speech, the emotional shift when the lights change.

That's what we aim for: technical excellence that serves the moment. We don't show off with unnecessary complexity or gear for the sake of gear. We match the equipment and design to the event — nothing more, nothing less.

Being a small team is our strength. When you hire SSBD, you work directly with us from the first email to the last cable wrap. There's no telephone game between sales, project management, and the crew on the ground. We are the crew. We know your event because we've been involved since day one.

This personal approach also means we're selective about the events we take on. We don't overcommit. Every event on our calendar gets our full attention, and we have the capacity to handle last-minute changes or creative requests without breaking a sweat.
