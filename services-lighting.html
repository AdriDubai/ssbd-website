Event Lighting Rental Dubai

Lighting is what transforms a venue from ordinary to extraordinary. The right combination of colour, intensity, and movement creates atmosphere, guides attention, and makes every moment more memorable. At Same Same But Different, we specialise in event lighting rental across Dubai and the UAE — from elegant uplighting for weddings to dynamic moving head programming for live shows and launches. Our lighting designers don't just set up fixtures — they design an experience. Using equipment from Martin, Chauvet Professional, and controllers like grandMA and Avolites, we craft lighting that complements your event's story. Whether it's a warm, intimate glow for a private dinner or a high-energy light show for a product reveal, we tailor every setup to match the space, the mood, and your vision.

What's Included

Our Lighting Equipment & Services

Moving Heads

Beam, spot, and wash moving heads from Martin and Chauvet — for dynamic effects, gobo projections, and dramatic stage looks.

LED Par Cans

Versatile LED pars for stage washes, colour accents, and general event lighting. Low heat, full colour mixing, and reliable performance.

Uplighting

Transform any venue wall, column, or architectural feature with colour. Perfect for weddings, galas, and corporate branding.

Architectural Lighting

Highlight building facades, outdoor landscapes, and venue features with precision-aimed fixtures for a polished, professional look.

Haze & Atmospheric Effects

Haze machines make light beams visible and add depth. Essential for any event where lighting is a key visual element.

Lighting Controller

All shows programmed and operated on grandMA or Avolites consoles by our experienced lighting designers for pixel-perfect control.
